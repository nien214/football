Mouse Soccer

Top view 2D soccer game, 3v3 only. Mouse only.

Connecting...

  • Move cursor: selected footballer follows cursor direction.
  • Arrow keys or W,A,S,D: move keyboard cursor and control movement.
  • Spacebar: pass/shoot in mouse or keyboard cursor direction.
  • Click footballer: select footballer.
  • Keys 1,2,3: select player. If your team has the ball, pass to player 1/2/3.
  • After goal: celebration and score board play automatically.
  • Ball owner click teammate: pass.
  • Ball owner click pitch: shoot to that direction.
